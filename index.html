Find Your Values.

The Way of the Essentialist involves doing less, but better, so that you can make the highest possible contribution to your projects, interests, and life. It's not about getting more done in less time, or about only getting less done. It's about getting only the RIGHT things done. It's about regaining control of your own choices about where to spend your time and energy, instead of giving others permission to choose for you.

The first step to The Way of the Essentialist is identifying your core values. You'll be given a list of values to rank from least to most important. Identifying your MOST important values is the MOST important step in doing less, better!

Crush Your Goals.

Once your most important values have been found, you can assign them to your projects. After this key step, you will be able to easily see what matters most in your life. This is the Way of the Essentialist, and it's how you're going to CRUSH your goals!
